The early years .....

As a young boy I was drawn to farming life, I only wanted to be working outside with the animals. I didn't have the good fortune where my parents having owning a farm so it soon became clear that my career would take a very different direction. At school all I was interested in was mathematics, looking back I was always keen to understand how electrical devices worked and on many occasions the devices never returned to working order.
My secondary school years were enjoyable and I was always just above average in my grades.

Sport
Hockey was my sport and I started at the age of 12 years old and finally retired playing at the grand old age of ~37.
